Hi, I'm Phillip Smith. I founded the Journalism Entrepreneurship Training Co. because I believe that small digital upstart news organizations will become the democracy-serving oases in a time of growing news deserts.

That's why I'm working to build workshops and intense trainings that help to bring news startups to life. My laser focus is working with diverse founders who are leaving newsrooms to start audience-pay information businesses.
